Mir Junaid

Research Engineer @ MesoNET

I received my master's degree in Computer Science and Engineering with a specialization in Computer Vision from VIT University, India in 2017. I worked on the ﻿classification of Images using Convolutional Neural Networks and received the best Research-Based Learning (RBL) award from VIT University in 2016. I am currently working towards my Ph.D. degree in Graph Machine Learning and Artififial Intelligence (AI) at the Laboratory of Modelling and Safety of Systems (LM2S), Universite de Technologie de Troyes(UTT), France. My Thesis Project is funded by the European Regional Development Fund (Fonds européen de développement régional). My research interests include Machine Learning, Graph Signal Processing, Geometric Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Big Data, Cloud Computing, Internet-of-Things (IoT), and Blockchains. I am also the President of the doctoral students association of UTT, France.
