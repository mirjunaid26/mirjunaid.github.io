Mir Junaid

Research Scholar

UTT, France

I am a Doctoral Student (PhD) at M2S Team of UTT, France. I am working on Geometric Deep Learning and Graph Signal Processing with Dr. Hichem Snoussi and Dr. Babiga Birregah. My Thesis Project is funded by European Regional Development Fund (FEDER) and Government of France (MINISTÈRE). I have obtained my Master's Degree in Computer Science and Engineering with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence from VIT University , India in 2017. I worked on the ﻿classification of Images using Convolutional Neural Networks and received the best Research-Based Learning (RBL) award from VIT University in 2016. Also, I did Bachelor's in Computer Science & Engineering from IUST, Kashmir in 2014. My research interests include Geometric Deep Learning, Graph Signal Processing, Machine Learning, Big Data, Computer Vision, Internet-of-Things (IoT), and Blockchains. Apart from my research, I am the President of ElliDoc, a Doctoral Association of UTT, France.
